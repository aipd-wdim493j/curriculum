Choose Your Own Adventure

  • Read the Readline documentation, paying close attention to the question(query, callback) section.
  • In the week2/homework/ directory, use the readline module to create a Choose Your Own Adventure with at least three (3) decision points.
  • Here is a barebones example: 
    $ node app.js
You wake up in a dimly lit room. Moonlight trickles through a small window.
You hear scratching ahead and notice an ornate door. Do you open the [door] or [window]?
> door
You approach the door and the scratching ceases. You slowly pull the door,
light fills the room and the smell of churducken assaults your nostrils.
A well-lit corridor lies ahead. Do you go [forward] or [vomit] from the smell?
> vomit
Not a fan of churducken? Well, clean yourself up then.
Do you want to try the [window] or go [forward] into the corridor?
> window
You unlock the window and slide it up high enough for large enough for a
fowl to fit through. An Undead Churducken attacks you. You have died.
